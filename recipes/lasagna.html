Homestyle Lasagna

Beautiful Lasagna recipe

This Lasagna is a family recipe that has been handed down through the generations. A luscious rague of minced Beef, silky pasta and depth throughout will leave you coming back for yet another bite. A family favourite in our household abd gaurantee this will be yours too once you have tried this.

For this recipe you will need the following.

Ingredients:

  • Minced Beef
  • Diced Onion
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • dried herbs
  • Pasta sheets
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Start by sweating down the onions in a little oil and then brown off the minced Beef.
  2. Add the tinned tomatoes and dried herbs and cook for a further 5 minutes.
  3. In a pan add the flour and milk to make a simple bechamel source, add your favourite cheese and mix in well.
  4. Now we begin to layer. Start with our beef mixture and then layer pasta sheets and a little bechamel sauce, repeat until all the mixture has gone.
  5. lastly add more Cheese to the top and bake in an oven at 180c for 1 hour
  6. Enjoy!!
