Asian stir-fry recipe

Authentic asian stir-fry recipe

This simple yet delicious stir-fry recipe is the perfect weeknight dinner. strips of Beef, Bell peppers, Mushrooms and Pak choi all cooked wonderfully with the aromatics of spring onion, Garlic and Ginger with a salty and savoury asian sauce will have you and the family coming back for seconds.

For this recipe you will need the folowing.

Ingredients:

  • Beef strips
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • spring onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Pak choi
  • Soy sauce
  • Dar soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Oyster sauce

Steps:

  1. Firstly marinate the Beef in a little soy and sesame oil and sear in a smoking hot pan or wok
  2. Set aside the Beef and start by cooking the aromatics. Ginger, Garlic and Spring onion go in.
  3. Then add the Bell peppers and Mushrooms followed by the Pak choi
  4. Make a simple marinade of Soy sauce, dark soy, oyster sauce and Sesame oil
  5. Lastly add the Beef back into the pan followed by the sauce to finish off cooking

There we have it, an authentic Asian stir-fry. Serve with white rice for a tasty no fuss weeknight meal.
